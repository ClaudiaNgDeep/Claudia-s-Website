About

Dr Claudia Ng Deep is a Christian Clinical Psychologist with a post-graduation degree in Clinical Neuropsychology. She has completed a Master's degree in Health Psychology and a Doctoral Degree in Psychology. She has worked in the mental health sector since 1998, in Portugal and in England, and has extensive experience delivering teaching, training, workshops, and clinical supervision on topics related to mental and physical health.

Dr Claudia Ng Deep uses different clinical therapeutic models to support clients in exploring physical, emotional, and relationship suffering, and in developing greater resilience. She is registered with the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC) and has published books and scientific articles.
