Privacy Notice

This privacy notice explains how information may be handled when you contact Dr Claudia Ng Deep through this website or by email.

Information You Provide

If you email to enquire about services, the information you provide may include your name, contact details, availability, and any information you choose to share about your reason for contacting. Please avoid sending sensitive clinical details by email unless they are necessary for the enquiry.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to respond to enquiries, arrange appointments, provide professional services where agreed, manage records, and meet legal, regulatory, safeguarding, and professional obligations.

Confidentiality

Clinical and enquiry information is treated confidentially. Information may only be shared where you have given permission, where the law requires or allows it, or where there is a serious concern about safety or safeguarding.

Website Data

This website does not include a contact form, payment processing, advertising tracking, or analytics scripts. It loads Google Fonts from Google servers, and the website host may collect standard technical logs such as IP address, browser type, requested page, and time of request for security and reliability.

Email Security

Email is convenient but may not be fully secure. If you prefer not to send sensitive information by email, use email only to request a safer method of communication.

Your Rights

Depending on your location and the nature of the information held, you may have rights to request access, correction, restriction, deletion, or a copy of personal information. Some clinical or legal records may need to be retained where professional obligations require it.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact ngdeep.claudia@gmail.com.
